Cookie Policy

We may Process your Personal Data by placing or reading Cookies and similar technologies. Our website uses cookies. A cookie is a piece of data stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. There are two types of cookies: session cookies and persistent cookies. Our website uses both of them. Session cookies are temporary cookies that remain in your browser or device until you leave the website; they are deleted from your device once you close the browser. Session cookies enable you to navigate on our website, to access the member area and to store some temporary information. Persistent cookies allow our website to remember your preferences and settings when you visit our website in the future. Persistent cookies expire after some period of time. The use of cookies in these ways is to improve the functionality of our website and your experience when using it. Cookies can be disabled by changing the settings on your browser. If you disable cookies, you will be unable to log in to your profile on our website, and you will be unable to use our products and services. By continuing to use our website without changing your settings, you consent to our use of cookies as described above.
